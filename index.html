Hi, my name is Grace


and I'm a GIS Technician. I have a B.A. in History from the University of Western Ontario, and favorite way to tell stories is through cartography. This is why I am now a recent graduate of the Fleming College GIS Applications Specialist Program and call myself a GIS Technician. During my education I studied many aspects of the technology and applications involved in GIS work, including but not limited to: ESRI ArcGIS Suite, Automation with Python, SQL & Database Management, Web Development, Spatial Analysis Techniques, Environmental Modeling, Remote Sensing, and Cartography. I had the opportunity to work for Ontario Parks through the college for my final project, creating a 3D model of infrastructure and utility assets in one of their provincial parks. Now, I'm continuing to hone these skills by building web apps, learning more about automating every day processes, and experimenting with data visualizations. If you want to get in touch, write to me on Twitter or email me.
